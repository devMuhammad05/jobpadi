FAQ

Frequently asked
questions

Everything you need to know about Jobpadi. Still stuck? Reach out and we'll help.

How does Jobpadi find jobs?

We scan 30+ job boards — Jobberman, MyJobMag, LinkedIn, Indeed and more — every hour. Each listing is checked for genuine, active openings, so expired posts and ghost listings never reach you.

What's the difference between plans?

Plans differ by how many job categories you follow and how many alerts you get a day. Free covers 1 category at 1/day; paid plans scale up to 10 categories and 20 jobs/day, with priority support and email digests on higher tiers.

Do you support email delivery?

Yes. Telegram is available on every plan. The Pro plan adds a daily email digest delivered at 8PM, so you get one clean roundup of the day's matches straight to your inbox.

What kind of jobs does Jobpadi find?

Categories span software, design, marketing, data, product and more — full-time, remote, hybrid and on-site roles across Nigeria and globally. You pick the categories that fit, and we only send what matches.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Anytime, in one click from your account — no forms, no waiting. You keep access until the end of your billing period, and you'll never be charged again after cancelling.
